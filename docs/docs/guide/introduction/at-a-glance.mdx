# ChainFiliates Overview

ChainFiliates combines three essential components to deliver a seamless partner management experience: ChainFiliates Core, ChainFiliates.js, and
ChainFiliates Programs.

ChainFiliates can be utilized in various scenarios, including:

- Referral marketing
- Community building
- Influencer marketing
- Partner programs
